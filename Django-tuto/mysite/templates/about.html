About Anupam Ice Cream

Serving happiness since 1985

Our Story

What started as a small family dream in 1985 has grown into the most beloved ice cream destination in town.

Anupam Ice Cream was founded by Mr. Anupam Sharma with a simple vision: to create the finest ice cream using traditional recipes and premium ingredients. Over the decades, we've remained committed to quality, freshness, and the joy that comes with every scoop.

Today, we're proud to serve three generations of families who have made us part of their sweetest memories.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our ice cream legacy

1985 - The Beginning

Started our first ice cream parlor with just 5 flavors and a dream to spread happiness.

1995 - Expansion

Opened our second location and introduced 15 new exotic flavors to our menu.

2005 - Innovation

Launched our signature sundae collection and custom ice cream cakes.

2015 - Modern Era

Introduced premium ingredients and seasonal specials while maintaining our traditional recipes.

2025 - Today

Continuing to serve the community with 25+ flavors and unwavering commitment to quality.
